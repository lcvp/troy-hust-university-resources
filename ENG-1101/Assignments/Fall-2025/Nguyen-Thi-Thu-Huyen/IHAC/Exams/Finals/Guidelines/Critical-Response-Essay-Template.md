# **CRITICAL RESPONSE ESSAY TEMPLATE (400-500 WORDS)**

**I. Introduction**

$1^{st}$ sentence - **Background / general information** to **introduce the topic area**

$2^{nd}$ sentence - **Introduce the article: Title, Author, (Year)**

**-Briefly summarize the main *overall* view of the author (one sentence)**

$3^{rd}$ sentence: - Thesis statement (e.g. This essay will respond to..... This essay is a response to ...... )

**II. Body**  
**1. Summary:** (about 100 words)/ 2pts

**2. Evaluation:**

* author (expert?/ wide knowledge?, source of the article (reliable?? Famous?)
* ***ARGUMENTS*** (convincing or not; agree/disagree?)
* ***EVIDENCES*** (Facts, statistics, expert testimony, observation: -reliable forms of evidences? Examples?
* language (formal/informal?)
* ***OVERALL TONE?*** (explain why do you think so?)
* writing strategies? (rhetoric question, intentional repetition, comparison and contrast?, etc.)

**3. Synthesis:** Putting things together: combining specific ideas given in the article with the knowledge in the 4 readings learnt in the course.

**Conclusion**

* Restate the author’s main ideas.
* Give a **brief summary of the main strengths and weaknesses** of the author and (based on this) which is stronger overall.
* Considering the views of the author, give your own comments on the topic. This can include your opinion/ recommendations/ final thoughts on the topic.
